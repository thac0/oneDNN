Detailed Description

Primitive descriptor for a reorder primitive.

Examples:
cnn_inference_int8.cpp, cpu_matmul_quantization.cpp, cpu_rnn_inference_f32.cpp, cpu_rnn_inference_int8.cpp, performance_profiling.cpp, and reorder.cpp.
