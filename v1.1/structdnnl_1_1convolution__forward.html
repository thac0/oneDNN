Detailed Description

Convolution forward propagation.

Implements descriptor, primitive descriptor, and primitive for the convolution forward propagation.

Examples:
cnn_inference_f32.cpp, cnn_inference_int8.cpp, cnn_training_f32.cpp, cpu_cnn_training_bf16.cpp, memory_format_propagation.cpp, and performance_profiling.cpp.
